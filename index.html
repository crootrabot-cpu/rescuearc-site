How the system works

Mounted ready. Thrown first. Support after impact.

  1. Stage it at elevation: the device lives at the tower or platform where the responder is already scanning the water.
  2. Grab and throw immediately: the compact body is meant to support a cleaner quarterback-style release than a bulky ring toss.
  3. Use the rack logic: multiple units stay mounted at the station so another throw is available without wasting time.
  4. Create support in the water: after impact, the victim gets visible flotation while the responder closes the gap.
